Why choose DataKom Mobile?

Our business mobile network is more independent than other providers - including DataKom:mobile and also three other main networks. This allows us to be more flexible, offer better deals and be more transparent by offering unbiased advice.

At DataKom, we pride ourselves on offering not only the best customer service, but we also work with you to come up with the best bespoke business mobile plan. You don’t have to sift through multiple providers and settle for products that you aren’t fully satisfied with - our telecom consultants will listen and then build a solution perfect for you.

DataKom:mobile is geared around flexibility, as we know no two businesses are the same. We offer a range of SIM only, and SIM and mobile phone plans tailored to you. And our Wales-based support team is a phone call away for when you need help.

How it works

We’ll review your current telecom bills, and take a look at your phone system, business broadband and line setup.

We’ll discuss your business needs and suggest a future-proof telecom solution to fit your requirements.

You’ll get a personalised quote outlining how we’ll improve your business telecoms, saving you time and money.

You’ll be in safe hands with our award-winning team of engineers, account managers and tech support.

Wales’ preferred business mobile phone provider

We are Wales’ fastest growing business-to-business telecom company working with companies in Wales and South West England. Having provided business mobile phone services since 2007, we’ve been trusted by many businesses - from small to large.

As well as providing account management for customers of DataKom Mobile - our own independent network, we also look after other providers’ contracts. In fact, even if your business mobile phone contract is with Vodafone, O2 or EE, as a DataKom customer, we’ll still manage your account at no extra charge.
